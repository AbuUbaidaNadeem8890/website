# AbuUbaida — Brand Package (Starting Concept)

This is a proposed starting point. Treat it as a draft to react to, not a final identity — adjust freely.

## 1. Brand Concept

**Positioning**: Modern modest/contemporary menswear-forward clothing brand, rooted in a personal name that signals heritage, trust, and craftsmanship — similar in spirit to how many premium clothing labels use a founder's name.

**Brand personality**: Understated, confident, quality-first, warm. Not loud streetwear; not luxury-cold minimalism — sits in between: "elevated everyday."

**Tagline options** (pick or iterate):
- "Crafted for Everyday."
- "Timeless. Tailored. Yours."
- "Wear it well."

## 2. Logo Concept

Two directions to prototype with a designer:

1. **Wordmark-led**: "AbuUbaida" set in a custom-tracked serif or humanist sans, all lowercase or small-caps, with a small monogram mark ("AU" ligature) that can stand alone on labels/buttons/social avatars.
2. **Monogram-led**: An interlocking "A/U" monogram as the primary mark (works at small sizes — embroidery, favicons), paired with the full wordmark for headers and packaging.

**Usage rules**
- Minimum clear space around the logo = height of the "A" on all sides.
- Do not stretch, rotate, recolor outside the approved palette, or add drop shadows/outlines.
- Monogram-only usage is permitted below 48px width; full wordmark required above that where space allows.
- Always sufficient contrast: use the light mark on dark backgrounds, dark mark on light backgrounds (never mid-tone busy backgrounds without a solid-color plate behind it).

## 3. Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Deep Charcoal | `#1C1C1E` | Logo, primary text, headers |
| Primary | Warm Off-White | `#F7F4EF` | Backgrounds, negative space |
| Accent | Olive / Sand | `#A98F63` | CTAs, price tags, highlights |
| Accent (secondary) | Terracotta | `#B4552C` | Sale badges, seasonal accents — use sparingly |
| Neutral | Stone Grey | `#8C8A85` | Secondary text, borders |
| Success | Muted Green | `#4B7355` | In-stock indicators, confirmations |
| Error | Muted Red | `#A5433A` | Out-of-stock, form errors |

Dark mode: swap Charcoal/Off-White roles (background `#1C1C1E`, text `#F7F4EF`), keep Olive accent unchanged for brand consistency, reduce Terracotta saturation slightly to avoid vibrating on dark backgrounds.

## 4. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Fallback stack | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display / Headings | **Fraunces** (serif, variable) | `"Fraunces", Georgia, serif` | Editorial, warm, slightly characterful — used for hero text, category headers |
| Body / UI | **Inter** | `"Inter", -apple-system, sans-serif` | Neutral, highly legible at small sizes for product cards, forms, admin UI |
| Numeric / Prices | Inter, tabular-nums | — | Ensures price columns align in cart/checkout |

Type scale (base 16px, ratio 1.25):
`12 / 14 / 16 / 20 / 25 / 31 / 39 / 49px`

## 5. Packaging & Photography Direction

- **Packaging**: matte kraft or off-white mailer boxes, single-color (Charcoal or Olive) monogram stamp, minimal tissue paper in Sand tone, no plastic where avoidable (aligns with "quality-first, understated" personality).
- **Product photography**: neutral Off-White or Stone backgrounds, soft natural light, consistent model framing (¾ and flat-lay pairs per product), true-to-color (no heavy filters) so customers trust sizing/color online.
- **Social/lifestyle imagery**: warmer, environmental shots (studio + outdoor), still restrained — no oversaturation.

## 6. Voice & Tone

- Clear, direct, respectful — product copy states fabric, fit, and care plainly before adjectives.
- Avoid hype language ("insane drop!!"); prefer confident simplicity ("A relaxed cotton shirt built for daily wear.").
- Size/fit guidance is always specific and honest (e.g., "Runs true to size; model is 6'0", wearing size M").
